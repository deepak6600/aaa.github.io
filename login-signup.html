Hardware Permission Information

Why might the command not work?

Sometimes, new Android versions block hardware permissions (like camera or microphone) for apps running in the background for security reasons. Even after giving permission during setup, the system might stop it later.

How to fix this?

To solve this, you need to reset the app's most powerful permission: the Accessibility Service.

1. Go to your child's phone **Settings → Accessibility → Installed/Downloaded Apps**.
2. Find **"FamToolApp"** or **"Child Accessibility Service"**.
3. If it's **ON**, please turn it **OFF** and then turn it **ON** again. If it's already **OFF**, just turn it **ON**.

This will refresh the permissions, and the recording commands should work correctly.
